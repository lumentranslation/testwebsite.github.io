Services

LumenTranslation

LumenTranslation offers top-quality, professional translation services for individuals and businesses of all sizes. Below is the comprehensive list of services that we offer.

Translation

Your translation needs are unique and may demand specific criteria, such as a particular language pair, specialized expertise, or a specific style (e.g., translating medical reports or converting financial documents from English to Quebec French).

We partner exclusively with skilled translators who are carefully chosen to meet these exact requirements. Each translator is a native speaker of the target language, ensuring the highest quality. Our services cover translations into and from English, as well as a wide range of language combinations tailored to your needs.

Localization

We take translation to the next level. Beyond just translating your text, game, application, or video subtitles, we ensure full localization. This means adapting currencies, time zones, numerical formats, cultural nuances, and more to align perfectly with the specific needs of your target market. Our goal is to make sure the translation resonates with your audience, wherever they are.

Transcription

Despite technological advancements, machines still fall short in accurately converting speech to text. Our team ensures your audio transcription is done with precision and tailored to your specific instructions—whether you want every hesitation and audible error included or omitted. You can provide us with any common audio format, in any language, and we’ll handle the rest.

Machine Translation Post-Editing

In an era where machine translation (MT) and AI have made significant strides in improving automated translations, LumenTranslation is staying up-to-date with these advancements. We now accept projects that require machine translation post-editing (MTPE) services. This means that we are ready to work on translations generated by MT, ensuring they meet your standards through careful human revision.
